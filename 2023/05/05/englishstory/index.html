A Story About Wind

Text

Sometimes, new wind is born. So people fell “soft wind blows my face”. But people don’t know wind will go someplace.
The wind goes to the flower sea, it becomes the spring wind. It brings warmth. The flower dance for it. The wind goes to the rain forest, it becomes the summer wind. It brings hotness, the leaves swing for it. The wind goes to the garden, it becomes the autumn wind. It brings cool, the fruits ripen for it. The wind goes to the snow ground, it becomes the winter wind. It brings cold, snowmen roll for it.
The wind visits 4 seasons.”What a beautiful world!”it thinks.

Questions

  1. How many seasons in the wind’s trip?
  2. In summer, where is the wind goes?

Translate

有时，新的风诞生了。有人会感觉到：“柔和的风拂过我的脸”。但是人们不知道风会去什么地方。
风去了花海，变成了春天的风。它带来了温暖，花朵为它而起舞。风去了雨林，变成了夏天的风。它带来了炎热，树叶为它而摇摆。风去了花园，变成了秋风。它带来了凉爽，果实为它而成熟。风去了雪地，变成了冬天的风。它带来寒冷，雪人为它而轻滚。
风参观了四季。“多么美丽的世界！”它想。
